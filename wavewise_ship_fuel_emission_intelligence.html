⚗ Full Model Transparency
Resistance Model: R_total = R_calm × (1 + k × H²)
— where k = 0.015 (empirical wave resistance coefficient)
— H = significant wave height in metres

Power Model: P = (R_total × v) / η_propulsive
— v = ship speed in m/s, η = 0.65 (2-stroke efficiency)

Fuel Consumption: FC = P × SFOC × 24 / 1e6
— SFOC = 175 g/kWh (Specific Fuel Oil Consumption)

CO₂ Emissions: CO₂ = FC × 3.206
— 3.206 tCO₂/t HFO (IPCC emission factor for Heavy Fuel Oil)

All relationships are proportional — this is a simplified first-principles model designed for education, planning, and decision support. Not certified for regulatory compliance. For regulatory CII/EEXI calculations use class-approved tools.
